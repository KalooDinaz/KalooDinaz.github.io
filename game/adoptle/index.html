What is Adoptle Game?

Adoptle Game is a charming word puzzle game inspired by the love for pets and the adoption process. Players are challenged to guess the names of adoptable pets in as few attempts as possible, making it a fun and heartwarming brain teaser for all ages.

How to Play Adoptle Game

  1. Start the game by clicking on the "Play" button.
  2. Guess the pet's name by entering your attempt into the input box.
  3. Use hints and feedback to refine your guesses.
  4. Keep trying until you find the correct name and win the round!

The fewer attempts you take, the higher your score will be. Challenge yourself and share your results with friends!
